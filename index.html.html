HYDRODASH @ SENTOSA

Welcome to Singapore's first floating water park!
Challenge yourself with an obstacle course similar to those you find in Ninja Warrior or Wipeout!

Highlights


Test your athleticism, balance and agility with the many different obstacles throughout the course


Make your visit during high tide to "cliff-jump" off a 3m platform or slide off a giant slide

To Prepare

Here are some things to take note of if you visit Hydrodash!
Bring along:

  • Towel
  • Extra change of clothes
  • Soap to wash up after the fun
  • Contact lens for those with myopia (spectacles are not allowed)

Lockers are available at the restroom 2 minutes away, small lockers go for $6 per 24 hours
However, you can bring along some essentials (e.g. towel, phone, water bottle, wallet), there are places to leave them on the beach at your own risk.

Getting There

From Harbourfront MRT station, take the Sentosa Express to Beach Station
Walk or take the Beach Shuttle to Palawan Beach
